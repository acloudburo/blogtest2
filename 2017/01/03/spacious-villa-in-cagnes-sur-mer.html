Spacious villa in Cagnes Sur Mer

france locations

Jan 3

When we plan big family reunion holidays in south France at the CÃ´te D'Azur we prefer a spacious villa in Cagnes Sur Mer (which actually belongs to a good friend of us).

The villa offers 6 bedrooms 4 of them are overlooking the garden with the large swimming pool. On the ground floor there is a modern kitchen with oven and steamer, American fridge, induction plates as well as a living room, TV room and office. Further there are outside terraces, barbecue and traditional pizza oven, pool house with shower and toilet, petanque field, tennis table and a car port.

The swimming pool is managed with a minimal amount of chemicals and salty sea-water, which results in a very soft and comfortable water feeling. It’s heated what is ideal for October holidays, when the temperature in the evening is not as warm anymore.
On the right the petanque field, as well as the pool house, with a warm shower and toilets.

Enough space to get the a big group together on a long table

There is nothing better in autumn than going for jogging in the early morning at the spectacular sea front (with almost no people). The sea front is easy reachable from the house by car in around 10 minutes.

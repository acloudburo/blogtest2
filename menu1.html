The Google Website “ Actiosn on Google” is a incredible useful resource in context of conversational bots.

As stated on their site

“Actions on Google let you build for the Google Assistant. Your integrations can help you engage users through Google Home today, and in the future, through Pixel, Allo, and many other experiences where the Google Assistant will be available.”

In the section “Design Principles and Methodology” the provide an overview of their recommended design process.


The main steps outlined are

  • Pick the right use cases
  • Create a persona
  • Write dialogs
  • Test it out
  • Build and iterate
